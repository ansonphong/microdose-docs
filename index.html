Gyros

Gyros are used to control values like turning a virtual dial by rotating your wrist.

Atlas

Controls the size of your head in VR. High Atlas makes objects look small and miniature. Low Atlas makes objects appear large and immersive.

Throw

Controls the distance that the Nozzle is thrown from the Wand. Low throw causes the Nozzle to be close to your face.

Time Dilation

Controls the speed of time globally. Minimum value is 0.01 or 1% regular speed. Maximum value is 2x speed.

Max Speed

Controls the maximum speed at which you move through space.
